\item Consider the initial value problem $\left\{\begin{array}{l}
y' = \exp(y) \\
y(0) = 1
\end{array}

\emph{Hint: $\exp(1)\approx2.718$, $\exp(1+\exp(1))\approx3.567$, $\dfrac{2.718+3.567}{2}=3.1425$}
